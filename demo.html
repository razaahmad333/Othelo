Othelo

it is two player board strategy game

players having red and green stones

player1 have red stones

player2 have green stones

GamePlay

player1 first put his red stones

on board where he is allowed to put

all green stones between newly placed red stone

and all his earlier red stones

diagnolly, vertically and horizontally are converted into red stone

Allowed Places For A Player1


all the red stones see around them

if they got any green stone

then they go to that direction until got an empty space

that empty space is allowed for player1

if in moving that direction player1

got his own red stones

then he stops and do not find any empty space

if any player do not find any empty space where he can place his stones

then he losts his chance

same is repeated for player2

player having more stones at end of game won the match
